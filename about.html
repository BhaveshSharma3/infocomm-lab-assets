About Infocomm Lab

Advancing research in wireless communication, sensing, and intelligent systems

Who We Are

The Infocomm Lab at IIT Mandi focuses on fundamental and applied research in wireless communication systems, signal processing, sensing, and intelligent networked systems.

Our work spans theory, simulation, and real-world experimental validation, with applications in indoor localization, human activity sensing, autonomous systems, and next-generation wireless networks.

Research Thrust Areas

Wireless Communication

Channel modeling, PHY-layer algorithms, and next-generation wireless systems.

Wireless Sensing

CSI-based sensing, indoor localization, human activity and object tracking.

AI for Communications

Machine learning and generative models for robust wireless systems and sensing.

Networked Autonomous Systems

Communication-aware control, drone swarms, and cyber-physical systems.

Experimental Facilities

The lab is equipped with software-defined radios, WiFi testbeds, multi-antenna platforms, and embedded systems for real-world experimentation.

We emphasize reproducible research and open experimentation, bridging the gap between theory and deployment.
